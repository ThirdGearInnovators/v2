STEM Program for Ages 9-11

Building Future Innovators

Our program for 9-11 year olds dives into intermediate projects in robotics, programming, and mechanical engineering with guided challenges. Students will work with Arduino, mBot, and Micro:bit kits to build practical skills.

Program Highlights

  • Monthly hands-on project kits with all materials included
  • Focus on robotics, programming, and mechanical engineering
  • Monthly in-person meetings for guidance and collaboration
  • Student journal for tracking progress and reflections
  • Builds on foundational skills with more complex challenges

Monthly Curriculum

Month Focus Kit Take-Home Work
October Maker mindset and design thinking MakeDo Cardboard Kit Build 2 structures; document process
November Electrical circuits and conductivity SmartLab Circuits Complete 3 circuit challenges
December Microcontrollers and programming ELEGOO UNO Kit Blink an LED; complete 2 beginner projects
January Applied engineering principles Thames & Kosmos Physics Workshop Build 3 guided models; document learnings
February Robotics and solar power OWI 14-in-1 Solar Robot Kit Build 3 different robot forms; compare movement
March Programming fundamentals Micro:bit v2 Starter Pack Program a thermometer or step counter
April Creative coding and 3D modeling Tinkercad + Scratch Extension Create a Scratch animation or 3D keychain
May Biotechnology and microscopy MEL Science DNA & Microscopy Kit Extract DNA from fruit; journal observations
June Showcase and celebration Choose your own capstone project Build or improve a previous project for demo

What's Included

  • 9 complete project kits with all necessary components
  • Monthly in-person guidance sessions with experienced instructors
  • Student journal for tracking progress and reflections
  • Access to online resources and support materials
  • Showcase event in June to present projects and celebrate achievements
  • All materials included - no additional purchases needed
