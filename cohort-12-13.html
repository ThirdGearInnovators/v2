STEM Program for Ages 12-13

Advanced Engineering & Technology

Our advanced program challenges 12-13 year olds with complex engineering projects, programming, and real-world problem solving. Students will work with Raspberry Pi, Arduino, and 3D printing to tackle sophisticated STEM challenges.

Program Highlights

  • Advanced project-based learning with real-world applications
  • Robotics, automation, and programming with Python
  • 3D design, CAD modeling, and 3D printing
  • Monthly hands-on project kits with all necessary components
  • Student portfolio development for future opportunities

Monthly Curriculum

Month Focus Kit/Project Take-Home Work
October Advanced robotics and automation Makeblock mBot Ranger Program 3 robot behaviors; document process
November Programming with Python Raspberry Pi 400 Kit Complete 3 Python projects using GPIO pins
December 3D design and printing Tinkercad + 3D Printer Access Design and print 2 functional objects
January Advanced electronics Arduino Starter Kit Build and program 3 sensor-based projects
February Renewable energy systems Renewable Energy Kit Design and test a solar-powered device
March Internet of Things (IoT) Raspberry Pi IoT Kit Create a working IoT device with cloud connectivity
April AI and machine learning Google Coral USB Accelerator Train and deploy a custom image classifier
May Engineering design challenge Engineering Design Kit Complete a complex engineering challenge
June Capstone showcase Capstone Project Present final project at showcase event

What's Included

  • 9 advanced project kits with all necessary components
  • Monthly in-person guidance sessions with expert instructors
  • Access to 3D printers and other advanced equipment
  • Student portfolio development and resume building
  • Showcase event in June to present projects to the community
  • All materials included - no additional purchases needed

Learning Outcomes

  • Advanced problem-solving and critical thinking skills
  • Hands-on experience with industry-standard tools and technologies
  • Experience with the complete engineering design process
  • Portfolio of completed projects for college applications
  • Team collaboration and project management experience
