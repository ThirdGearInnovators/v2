STEM Program for Ages 6-8

Inspiring Young Makers Through Hands-On Learning

Our program for 6-8 year olds introduces foundational STEM concepts through engaging, hands-on projects that make learning fun and interactive. This cohort focuses on hands-on exploration of basic engineering, simple circuits, and creative problem-solving through play-based activities.

Program Highlights

  • Monthly hands-on project kits with all materials included
  • Focus on engineering, circuits, and creative problem-solving
  • Monthly in-person meetings for guidance and collaboration
  • Student journal for tracking progress and reflections
  • No prior experience needed - perfect for beginners

Monthly Curriculum

Month Focus Kit Take-Home Work
October Electrical circuits and conductivity Snap Circuits Green Energy Build 2 circuits; reflect on "green" impact
November Intro to robotics and block coding mBot Explorer Complete tutorial and line-follow mod
December Mechanical engineering KiwiCo Mechanical Claw Enhance claw for force or reach
January Physical computing micro:bit Starter Kit Create 2 projects (thermometer, quiz)
February Smart home technology Elecfreaks Smart Home Build 2 smart systems, create pitch
March Robotics and sensors LEGO SPIKE Essential Assemble robot and sensor routines
April Wearable technology Tech Will Save Us Wearable Kit Build wearable, add switch or light
May Mechanical engineering KiwiCo Chain Reaction Create unique chain reaction, video
June Showcase and celebration Remix Kit + Showcase Prep Final project and presentation prep

What's Included

  • 9 complete project kits with all necessary components
  • Monthly in-person guidance sessions with experienced instructors
  • Student journal for tracking progress and reflections
  • Access to online resources and support materials
  • Showcase event in June to present projects and celebrate achievements
  • All materials included - no additional purchases needed
