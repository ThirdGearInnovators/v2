Support Future Innovators

Your Donation Fuels Hands-On STEM Education

At Third Gear Innovators, we believe that hands-on experience is the best way to inspire and prepare the next generation of engineers, mechanics, and problem-solvers. Your donation helps provide tools, materials, and mentorship for students eager to learn STEM through real-world projects like building kit cars.

🚀 How Your Donation Helps

Every contribution—big or small—makes a direct impact on our mission to bring STEM to life for students of all backgrounds.

  • $25 – Provides essential tools for students in our workshops.
  • $50 – Helps fund materials like wiring, fasteners, and structural components.
  • $100+ – Supports full workshop sessions and student scholarships.
