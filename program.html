9-Month STEM Program

Hands-On Learning for Young Innovators (Ages 6-13)

Our 9-Month STEM Program is designed to inspire the next generation of innovators through hands-on, project-based learning. Students will explore engineering, coding, and design thinking while working on exciting, real-world projects.

  • Register Now

Age Cohorts

Ages 6-8

Foundational skills in building, basic circuits, and problem-solving through hands-on, play-based projects with Snap Circuits and LEGO SPIKE Essential kits.

Learn More →

Ages 9-11

Intermediate projects in robotics, programming, and mechanical engineering with guided challenges using Arduino, mBot, and Micro:bit kits.

Learn More →

Ages 12-13

Advanced projects in electronics, coding, and engineering with real-world applications using Raspberry Pi, Arduino IoT, and 3D printing technologies.

Learn More →

Program Highlights

  • 9-month program (October 2024 - June 2025)
  • Monthly hands-on sessions with take-home projects
  • Small class sizes (max 10 students per cohort)
  • All program materials and kits included
  • Student journal for tracking progress and reflections

Program Schedule

October 2025 - June 2026
Monthly sessions on Saturdays or Sundays
Location: Local library (exact location TBD)

Program Cost

Thanks to a generous grant from a local tech company, this program is being offered at no cost to participants.
