STEM Program for Ages 6-8

Inspiring Young Makers Through Hands-On Learning

Our program for 6-8 year olds introduces foundational STEM concepts through engaging, hands-on projects that make learning fun and interactive. Each month, students receive a carefully curated kit designed to spark curiosity and develop problem-solving skills.

Program Highlights

  • Monthly hands-on project kits delivered to your door
  • Focus on engineering, circuits, and creative problem-solving
  • Monthly in-person meetings for guidance and collaboration
  • Designed for young learners with no prior experience needed

Monthly Curriculum

Month Focus Kit
October Creative Building & Design Thinking KiwiCo "Playful Structures"
November Simple Machines KiwiCo "Spin Art Machine"
December Basic Circuits Snap Circuits Jr. SC-100
January Magnetism and Motion TinkerCrate "Magnetic Levitation"
February Sensors and Sound LittleBits Electronic Music Inventor Kit
March Renewable Energy Thames & Kosmos Wind Power Set
April Motion and Mechanics LEGO Technic™ "Pull Back Racer"
May Visual Coding Osmo Coding Starter Kit
June Capstone Project KiwiCo Light-Up Planetarium

What's Included

  • 9 complete project kits (one per month)
  • Monthly in-person guidance sessions
  • Access to online resources and support
  • Showcase event in June to celebrate achievements
