9-Month STEM Program

Hands-On Learning for Young Innovators (Ages 6-13)

Our 9-Month STEM Program is designed to inspire the next generation of innovators through hands-on, project-based learning. Students will explore engineering, coding, and design thinking while working on exciting, real-world projects.

  • Register Now

Age Cohorts

Ages 6-8

Foundational skills in building, basic circuits, and problem-solving through play and art-based projects.

Learn More →

Ages 9-11

Intermediate projects in robotics, programming, and mechanical engineering with guided challenges.

Learn More →

Ages 12-13

Advanced projects in electronics, coding, and engineering with real-world applications.

Program Highlights

  • 9-month program (October 2024 - June 2025)
  • One 45-minute in-person session per month
  • Small class sizes (max 10 students per cohort)
  • All program materials and kits included
  • Led by experienced instructors from our nonprofit
